Our Product

Our app

The PeerPark app is a free and very user-friendly application that allows users to rent a parking spot very quickly. Whether you're living in Toronto or Los Angeles, if you're in desperate need of a parking spot all it takes is a couple of minutes

Renting a Parking spot

If you're in need of a parking spot all it takes is 3 very simple steps:

  • First you nee to find the location where you want to park your vehicle
  • Then you need to find and select an ad/posting (you'll see more information about the parking spot once ad has been selected)
  • Finally, you'll need to add all the relevant information (How long you'll be renting the parking spot, card number, etc.)
  • And that's it, you've now got yourself a parking spot and all it took were 3 steps!

Posting an ad

If you've got an extra parking spot and are looking to make extra money then all you need to do is follow these 3 simple steps:
Download and create an account in our mobile app
Take a picture of the parking spot and then add any important information like the price, how long the parking spot will be available for rent, etc.
And the final step is to post your add and that's it you've offcially posted you first ad using the PeerPark app in just 3 steps
