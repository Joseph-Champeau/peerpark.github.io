Our Product

Our app

The PeerPark app is a very user-friendly application that allows users to rent a parking spot very quickly. Whether your living in Toronto, Los Angeles or in Chicago, if you're in desperate need of a praking spot all it takes is a couple of minutes to get yourself a parking spot

Renting a Parking spot

If your're in need of a parking spot all it takes is:

  • Find the location where you want to park your vehicle
  • Find and select an ad/posting (you'll see more information about the parking spot once ad has been selected)
  • Insert all the relevant information (How long you'll be renting the parking spot, card number, etc.)
  • And that's it, you've now got yourself a parking spot and all it took were 3 steps!
