
Overall Look and Feel of Peerpark
==================================

The overall look and feel should be minimalist. We will avoid 3d metal looks and add certain 
effects/decorations to highlight certain objects.The website should be have a general 
city feel but muted to be more calming and pleasing to the eye.

Colors
=======
For Peerpark, the colors are those of our logo with variations to give a somewhat city feel as well as a calm and pleasing feel.
These are our color swatches:

- #676A74 -> gray 
- #7396AD -> blue-gray 
- #7ABD6C -> green 
- #BDF2E7 -> light blue 
- #4C6DBD -> dark blue

*Note: We might use colors that slightly vary from our colors swatches (darker, lighter and/or more blue/green)

Iconography
===========
We will focus on using icons that have a simple and "flat" design, which adheres to the overall look and feel.

Typography
==========

Links
=====
Links will be underlined and should change color when a user hovers over it.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be accompanied with a shadow and designed using the chosen colors outlined above and white
They will have a small outline and will change their color when a user hover over them.
