Our Prices

Rent

The prices of the rent for parking are decided by the users that are uploading listing. Prices varies depending on the location, time and users discretion.

Service

For using our application, we will collect a service fee of 5%. This fee is deducted off the price of the rent.

Membership

Though using our application is free, we offer a membership service.

  • Price: 10 $/month
  • Description: With this membership, we won't deduct a service off the pay from the rent fee and a discount on any rent.
